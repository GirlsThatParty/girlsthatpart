Girls That Party is a San Diego based company that plans any and all parties catered to women, specializing in bachelorette parties. We send each Bride a questionnaire that gives us a better idea of what she likes to do so the weekend is tailored to them. We will go over budgets and customize the trip so it is affordable for each lady and can work with any size party.
